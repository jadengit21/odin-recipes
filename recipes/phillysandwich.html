Philly Cheesesteak Sandwich

Description

A Philly cheesesteak sandwich features thinly sliced, sautéed beef piled onto a soft hoagie roll,
topped with melted provolone or Cheez Whiz. Often accompanied by sautéed onions, bell peppers,
and mushrooms, the sandwich is savory, hearty, and packed with rich, indulgent flavors—a true classic from Philadelphia.

Ingradients

  • Ribeye steak (thinly sliced)
  • Cheese (traditional provolone, American cheese)
  • Sub roll
  • Bell peppers
  • Mushrooms
  • Oil and butter
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Sauté the onions, bell peppers and mushrooms. I usually cook sliced onion first, after 3min,
    put peppers and mushrooms into the skillet.
  2. Cook the steak. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Melt the cheese. Layer cheese on the top of the meat, let it melt for minutes.
  4. Make the sandwich.Place all the mixed ingradients in to a sub roll or hoagie roll.
  5. Enjoy your tasty sandwich.
